Azenhas do Mar

Azenhas do Mar is a breathtaking coastal village in Portugal, perched dramatically on cliffs overlooking the Atlantic. Its whitewashed houses cascade down the rock face, creating a postcard-perfect scene. Known for its natural seawater pool, stunning ocean views, and rich history, it’s a hidden gem near Sintra. Azenhas do Mar offers a tranquil escape with a touch of Mediterranean charm.
